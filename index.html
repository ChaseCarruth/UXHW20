Research

Grow Coffee App was designed to create a platform to maximize the capabilities of at home coffee makers. The 2020 pandemic made it more difficult for people to visit coffee shops, and most coffee at home just doesn't taste the same. We realized that we needed a professional platform for at home users to learn how to make better specialty drinks and coffee at home.

Go somewhere

Qualitative data was collected through five user interviews. This research lasted two days to cover data collection. An online survey derived from our research plan was conducted through Google Forms.Participants consisted of a wide range of coffee drinkers with varying work schedules. Through this research we discovered that users were highly interested in making better coffee at home would greatly value the ability to have their drinks be more similar to barista quality.

Grow Coffee App was designed to provide a platform where users educate, inspire, and share knowledge for making the best quality coffee at home. Our research revealed that users care about the quality and taste of their coffee, but many users find it difficult to recreate barista quality coffee at home. As the at-home workforce grows, there's a need for users to learn how to utilize the equipment they have and learn new coffee making techniques, because misguided actions and lack of experience can lead to increased frustration, irritability, and wasting money.

How might we create Grow Coffee App so that users can be successful in making great coffee without wasting effort, time, or resources while growing the at home coffee community?

After evaluating user needs we began wireframing and rough designs of the grow app. While going through this process we kept this user insight statement in mind.

As the at-home workforce grows, coffee lovers need to learn how to utilize the equipment they have, while staying on schedule, to make cafe quality coffee because misguided actions can lead to increased frustration, irritability, and wasting money.

Finaly after much iteration and product testing we had a final prototype. We went with a sleek black design and a multi swipe layout to sell our idea of a professional level coffee tutorial app.
