Grow Coffee App

Growing the at home coffee community one cup at a time

  • Research
  • Design
  • Final Prototype

Grow Coffee App was designed to create a platform to maximize the capabilities of at home coffee makers. The 2020 pandemic made it more difficult for people to visit coffee shops, and most coffee at home just doesn't taste the same. We realized that we needed a professional platform for at home users to learn how to make better specialty drinks and coffee at home.
